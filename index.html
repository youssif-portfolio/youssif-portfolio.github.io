Projects that moved the needle

01
AI + Analytics

Instant Quotation Pricing Model — Rawmart

Building an NLP + ML model from scratch on data collection till serving to automate product pricing and reduce a manual, time-intensive process by ~60% of daily received products to price

Pricing incoming product requests was a slow, repeatitive manual process. The team had to look up product names and prices across scattered sources, creating bottlenecks and inconsistencies. And business wanted to increase the daily capacity of receieved products

Internal product catalog, historical transaction pricing data, supplier records, structured product input sheets, quottations from different sources like Images, PDFs and Sheets

automatic model data feeding from different sources and NLP + ML model to understand and match product names and fetch prices automatically. Built a Vector Database (Pinecone), deployed via Python Flask API on AWS RDS, with GPT API integration for classification tasks.

Sales, supply & operations teams handling inbound quotation requests daily.

📌

Business Impact

Pricing process time reduced by approximately 60%. The model went from concept to production deployment — handling real quotation requests in-house and eliminating the manual lookup bottleneck.

Python NLP (spaCy, NLTK) Vector Databases LLM API AWS RDS Hugging Face
02
Dashboard Build

Executive Supply Chain Dashboards — L'Oréal Egypt

ETL pipeline + 4 live Power BI dashboards for the outbound transportation team, replacing manual daily Excel entries

The outbound transportation team tracked daily shipment operations in a master Excel worksheet filled with manual entries — time-consuming, error-prone, and offering no real-time visibility.

Daily operations master worksheet, shipment records, customer data, port records across multiple Excel sources.

Cleaned and transformed master worksheet data, automated manual entry formulas, built a data pipeline across multiple Excel sheets, then developed 4 live Power BI dashboards covering lead times, delays, capacity monitoring, and customer/port performance.

Supply chain outbound transportation team and Executive management.

📌

Business Impact

First ever data visibility with Four live dashboards delivered with live refresh, eliminating manual tracking time. The team gained first-time real-time visibility into shipment lead times, delay patterns, and capacity utilization — enabling faster operational decisions.

Power BI Power Query Excel ETL DAX

BI Platform + Automation

Building and deploying a full business intelligence platform with SQL-based dashboards, automated data pipelines, and KPI tracking from the ground up

Transaction database, customer records, order data, financial data, online sales funnel events etc.. — modeled into analytical layer.

Built the startup's entire analytical layer, deployed Metabase as the in-house BI platform, and delivered dashboards covering all business teams Sales, Supply & Demand, Marketing, Product & Tech, Executives & Strategy

Founders, leads, operations team, sales and marketing leads, and finance.

📌

Business Impact

The startup went from zero data visibility to a fully operational BI platform. Daily, weekly, and monthly reports ran automatically. Stakeholders across functions had consistent, reliable KPI tracking for the first time — directly supporting funding and operations planning decisions.
